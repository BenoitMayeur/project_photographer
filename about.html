Personal details

This website is not finished and bas been put online to demonstrate my skills as a web developper.
I'm a professional photographer from Russia. I lived in Ireland, Spain and then moved to the French par of Belgium. I learned several languages and became fluent in each of them. I worked on photography projects in several countries and I know how hard it can be to have to translate explanations in a foreign language.

Photoshooting is a real act of creation. Finding the right angle is mostly about technique and inventivity, but transfering to a model what she or he has to do is a real challenge: the language and the translation of the feelings are then the keys.
